Northstar Workshop - Internal Support Policy

This fictional policy is provided only for the local Knowledge Base / RAG demo.

Equipment requests

Employees submit equipment requests through the internal support form. The
Workplace Operations team approves or rejects a complete request within three
business days. Requests above 1,500 demo credits also require Finance review.

Reimbursement requests

The employee's direct manager reviews reimbursement requests first. Finance
reviews a request only after manager approval. Employees must submit receipts
within ten calendar days of the purchase.

Rejected requests

If a request is rejected, the employee should read the rejection reason,
correct any missing information, and submit a new request. Urgent safety-related
equipment issues should be escalated to Workplace Operations instead of waiting
for a resubmission.

Leave requests

Planned leave of three or more working days should be submitted at least seven
calendar days before the first day of leave. The direct manager records the
decision in the internal leave system.
